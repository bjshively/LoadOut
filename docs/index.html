LoadOut

Save and restore your window layouts with one click. A free macOS menu bar utility.

Download for Mac

Requires macOS 13.0 (Ventura) or later

▢

Save Window Layouts

Capture the exact position and size of all your windows across multiple displays.

♻

One-Click Restore

Instantly restore your saved layouts. Apps launch automatically if they're not running.

⚙

Menu Bar Access

Quick access from your menu bar. Apply presets without switching apps.

⌨

Keyboard Shortcuts

Global shortcuts let you apply your first 9 presets from anywhere.

▶

Launch Items

Open apps, files, and URLs automatically when you apply a preset.

▤

Multi-Display Support

Works seamlessly with multiple monitors. Windows restore to the correct screen.

How It Works

01

Arrange Your Windows

Set up your apps and windows exactly how you like them.

02

Save as Preset

Select the apps you want to include and save with a memorable name.

03

Restore Anytime

Click your preset from the menu bar or use a keyboard shortcut. Done.

Global Keyboard Shortcuts

⌃ Control + ⌥ Option + 1-9

Apply your first 9 presets instantly from any app.

Privacy & Permissions

LoadOut requires Accessibility permissions to read and control window positions. This is handled entirely on your Mac — no data is ever sent anywhere. The app is open source, so you can verify this yourself.
